== Description ==
# Accept payments with Acquired.com

The Acquired.com for WooCommerce extension enables your business to securely accept payments by Card, Apple Pay & Google Pay to help you generate more sales.

== Features ==

<ul>
    <li>Pay by Card: securely accept Visa, Mastercard & American Express using EMV 3-D Secure (version 2.2)</b></li>
    <li>Digital Wallets: add Apple Pay & Google Pay to the Product, Cart or Checkout pages of your store to deliver a seamless checkout experience</b></li>
    <li>Customisable Checkout:</b> match your brand with full control to style the payment form to meet your requirements</li>
    <li>Saved Cards:</b> enable faster checkout for returning customers by letting them reuse previously stored cards</li>
    <li>Manage your payments:</b> update the payment statuses from within WooCommerce</li>
    <li>Always available: excellent customer service and reliability (99.999% uptime) at our core</li>
    <li>PCI DSS: remain fully compliant with the lowest amount of effort (SAQ-A) using our hosted solution</li>
</ul> 

== Quick & Easy Setup ==

Please [contact us](https://acquired.com/contact) to get a test account so you can try out the extension. 

<ol>
    <li>Access your WordPress dashboard.</li>
    <li>Select <b>Plugins</b> from the left navigation menu followed by <b>Add New</b></li>
    <li>Search for <b>Acquired.com</b> using the top right-hand corner search box</li>
    <li>Select <b>Install Now</b></li>
    <li>Once Installed you can enable and configure all things payments in the WooCommerce settings</li>
<li>Configure a few basic details and you are up and running</li> 
</ol>

Read our documentation [here](https://docs.acquired.com/docs/woocommerce-1) and our support teams are on hand to help with any questions you might have so please [contact us](https://acquired.com/contact).
